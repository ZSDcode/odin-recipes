How To Make Mashed Potatoes!

Description

Below is the recipe for making some of your most delicious mashed potatoes! Mashed potatoes can be used in almost any occasion as a nice addition. Thus, today, we'll be teaching you how to make some today!
Just follow along~

Ingredients List!

  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Butter (Add to your own discretion)
  • Milk (Half and half or half and heavy cream to amp things up!)
  • Cream Cheese (For an extra tinge)
  • Fine sea salt
  • Toppings (Optional)

Steps!

This is where the magic happens:

  1. Cut the potatoes. Up to you on whether the skins should be left on them or not but cut evenly.
  2. Boil the potatoes. About 10 to 12 minutes until the potato becomes soft. At this stage, you can also add some salt to season it up!
  3. Prepare your Butter mixture! Crucially, Heat your butter and milk till the butter melts and set it aside.
  4. Pan-dry the Potatoes. Using the same pot, now lower the heat, remove the water and allow the potatoes to evaporate off some excess water.
  5. Mash the potatoes. Mash to your liking!
  6. Stir everything together! Taking your melted butter, stir into the potatoes slowly, folding the potatoes over . But don't overmix, slow and steady is the trick!
  7. Taste and season! You're almost done, now just tuning and adding of toppings or more salt.
  8. Time to serve!
